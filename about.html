ABOUT JADOO

Our Story, Vision, and Mission

OUR STORY

After more than 20 years of experience in the Saudi Arabia's Human Capital Development market, JAD Consulting (JADCO) was established to continue supporting the industry with a new inspired vision by the great Saudi Vision 2030.

JADCO and its highly ranked international partners of Companies, Universities and SMEs are forming together an exclusive and innovative consortium to serve and be part of the revolution and development and support the transformation for the next levels.

JADCO in collaboration with the best partners in the globe, customize and Tailor projects to bridge the gap and providing the latest technologies to ensure the max level of quality of deliverables, support local content and transform knowledge to meet the objectives of our clients.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative solutions in education, technology, and entertainment, transforming the way people learn, work, and interact with the digital world.

We envision a future where education and technology seamlessly integrate to create meaningful experiences that empower individuals and organizations to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional services and solutions that bridge the gap between education and innovation, supporting the transformation of Saudi Arabia and beyond in line with Vision 2030.

We are committed to excellence, continuous improvement, and creating lasting value for our clients, partners, and communities through our diverse portfolio of services.
